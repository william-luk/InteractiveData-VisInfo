This is a visualization of shots taken under 5 or less seconds on the game clock during the 4th quarter (last quarter of regulation time). The data represents the 2014-15 NBA regular season from November to February. Using Basketball Reference and the dataset from DanB's Kaggle NBA shot logs, this is a graphical way to view the actual distances and positions shots were taken from, as the court is scaled to real dimensions. Click on the shot markers to jump to the related information. Green markers indicate a made shot, Red markers indicate a missed shot. Sort the shot information by month and shot type using top bar links.
Created by William Luk, Visualizating Information 2 Modeling. Made with pure JS, CSS and Anime.js
Optimized for maximized desktop window viewing.
